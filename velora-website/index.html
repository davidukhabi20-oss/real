Who We Are

Our Belief in Nigeria's Potential

Nigeria possesses everything required to build globally respected food manufacturing businesses: fertile land, hardworking farmers, and entrepreneurial talent.

Velora exists to connect these strengths through value addition, modern manufacturing, and disciplined execution. We are not building for the next quarter; we are building a company that stands the test of time to ensure food sovereignty for Nigeria.
